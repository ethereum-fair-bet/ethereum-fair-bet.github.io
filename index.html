Welcome to Ethereum Fair Bet

These contracts allow you to bet Ethereum with other users.

Your chances to win are always 50/50 and are based on unpredictable hashes of block chain.

You can bet as many times as you want at once.

Three possible amounts are available (0.01, 0.05 and 0.10 ETH).

Bet [ETH] Contract address Payload
0.01 0x12345678901234567890 0x12345
0.05 0x12345678901234567890 0x12345
0.10 0x12345678901234567890 0x12345

Contract fee is around the gas level on 0.001 ETH for each bet.

Chances to win

To determine winner contract uses hashes next after second user transaction.

Example:

If the first user bet in block number 3883654 and second user bet in block number 3883657, contract will use hash of 3883658.

Then it checks last bit of this hash. If it is 1 first user win. If it is 0 second user win.

So in our exaple, due to hash of 3883658 block, which is 0xe736ca63d82769091e10e73f1fee3b19bf2609e48d51a29281f96a2dd976a138, last bit is 0 and second user wins. 

How to bet

To bet you have to simply send expected amount to contract address.

It is possible to make as many bets as you want.

All of them will be waiting for other users to bet.

You cannot play with yourself (unless you play from two addresses).
